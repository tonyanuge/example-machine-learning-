Features:

Hate Less. To detect hate speech on twitter.

This plugin is compatible with the Twitter post feed. 
Because current posts do not contain abusive content, 
you must look for previous posts containing abusive or
hate speech and bookmark them in order to test the plugin.

To connect and run the pluin.

Step 1: On your IDLE Run python server.py in the terminal. 

Step 2: Open your twitter bookmarked feed.

Step 3: Upload the plugin to Chrome extension and turn on.

Step 4: Go back to the bookmarked feed and refresh the page to view the software effect on its content.
